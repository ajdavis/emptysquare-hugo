LeaseGuard is a new enhancement to the Raft consensus protocol that strengthens Raft's consistency guarantees and time-to-recovery. I developed LeaseGuard with my MongoDB colleagues Murat Demirbas and Lingzhi Deng, and our paper is in SIGMOD 2026. Here's [a summary of the protocol](/leaseguard-raft-leader-leases-done-right).
