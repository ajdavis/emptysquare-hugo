My [sangha](https://npzc.org/) held its first sesshin, a Zen meditation retreat. We sat in our stone cabin with a fire in the hearth. At mealtimes we went down the hill to the abbot's house to eat in the Zen oryoki style. On the last day two members took jukai (the Buddhist precepts) and received dharma names.
